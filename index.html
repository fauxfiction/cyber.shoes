INSTALLING CYBER.SHOES

Clone the CYBER.SHOES repo from git@github.com:Jaiz909/cyber.shoes.git Open Chrome, navigate to the settings menu and click Extensions.

Drag CYBER.SHOES.crx onto the Extensions page.
